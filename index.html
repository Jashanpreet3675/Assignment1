Biography

MILKHA SINGH (20 November 1929 â€“ 18 June 2021), known by his nickname THE FLYING SIKH, was an Athlete. He became the first Indian male to reach the final of an Olympic athletics event when he placed fourth in the 400-metre race at the 1960 Olympic Games in Rome. At the 1958 Asian Games, Singh won both the 200-metre and 400-metre races.
